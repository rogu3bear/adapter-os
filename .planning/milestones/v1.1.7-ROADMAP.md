## Overview

This milestone modernizes adapter operations into a git-like version-control workflow and makes dataset-fed evolution first-class in Update Center. It reuses existing adapter repository/version primitives and training dataset ingestion paths; scope is UX semantics, route/client clarity, and phase-accurate documentation reconciliation.

`GOV-16` remains accepted external debt from v1.1.5 and is out of scope for v1.1.7.
